RD Cafe

Rajan Dhakal

Nestled in the heart of the city, RD Cafe isn’t just a place to enjoy a coffee—it’s an experience that brings people together. Founded by Rajan Dhakal, RD Cafe began as a dream to create a cozy space where friends, families, and even strangers could connect over a cup of perfectly brewed coffee.

Our story started with a simple idea: to serve coffee that’s as rich in flavor as it is in history. Rajan’s passion for artisanal coffee led him to explore unique brewing techniques and source the finest beans from local farmers, ensuring that every cup reflects the love and dedication we have for this craft. From our meticulously selected beans to the warm ambiance that fills the cafe, every detail has been crafted to make you feel at home.
